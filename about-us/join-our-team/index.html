Employee Stories

We support your growth

There's plenty of room to grow within the MA community. We're committed to hiring diverse talents and providing the right opportunities for professional and personal growth. Our Data Academy and Communities of Practice provide a robust platform for continuous improvement, to meet your personal development aims, and the requirements of the market.

We care about Diversity and Inclusion

We celebrate diversity and acknowledge that our differences enrich both our business and individual experiences. We strive to make Methods Analytics an inclusive, happy and rewarding place to work for all colleagues, with a work environment where people can thrive and everyone feels valued.  We do this through the sustained commitment of our Senior Leadership Team, and our volunteer-led improvement group, MACI (Methods Analytics Continuous Improvement).
