About US

Our Story

Being a market leader in Telecom and focusing on the BFSI industry supporting Greenfield Implementation Transformation, and Managed Services. Celcom has extensive hands-on experience in delivering quality services for global clients. We are an ISO 9001-2015 certified company that helps organizations with Implementation, Solutions, and Consulting Services for the Oracle BSS/OSS product stack and Digital transformation, testing, and data services 

Founded in 2010 in the UK and having regional offices in India (Chennai & Bengaluru) and the USA (Atlanta), we bring years of domain expertise and global experiences to play. Our focus has been on offering the right portfolio of Products and Services to our clients across different verticals.

At Celcom, we have deep expertise in the Telecom domain and a strong exposure to BFSI vertical with a global footprint across multiple geographies, including APAC, MENA, the Middle East, and the UK, we have been able to deliver our clients industry-specific solutions in digital transformation, testing, data services, domain-led OSS/BSS stack services, and consulting.

We celebrate your wins

Since our foundation, we have operated on the principle of bringing more value to customers by building and executing effective technological solutions that fit their needs and drive the progress they desire. Over the years, we have provided industry-specific solutions in digital transformation, testing, data services, 5G integration transformation services, domain-led services for OSS/BSS stack, and consulting. At Celcom, we are driven by a strong passion for facilitating our customers to build stronger, agile, and innovative businesses, and our primary focus has been to deliver solutions that could transform the customer's user experience. This has been possible because of our industry practice, strong leadership, unique methodology, subject matter expertise, and decades of global experience. We constantly strive to upgrade and innovate to maximize and sustain our value for each stakeholder.
